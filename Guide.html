InvoiceXtractor

It is a powerful tool designed to extract essential information on Invoices. It can extract key details from invoices, including the Invoice Number, Invoice Date, Purchase Order, Total Dues, and Payment Terms.

How to Execute:
  1. Select a brand from the drop-down list
  2. Select a date
  3. Click “Download” Attachment under the Invoice Parser Tool
  4. Click "Parser Now" under the Invoice Parser Tool
  5. Wait until the ‘success GIF appears’, and then click "Close"
NOTE:
    Ensure that you copy the data to your Excel file in the desired location before clicking "Clear" to empty the columns. Otherwise, if you re-execute the process, the data will be overwritten.
